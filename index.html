CLIMATE 200

Climate 200 is an Australian initiative that supports political candidates prioritizing climate action and gender equity, aiming to influence government policies through strategic campaigns and community involvement.

LEARN MORE

RESTORE BLUE

Restore Blue aims to restore and protect coastal wetlands, such as saltmarshes and mangroves, which provide valuable ecosystem services and store "blue" carbon, making them a vital part of the climate solution. They collaborate with global partners and use the best-available science and rigorous legal standards to restore a minimum of 10,000 hectares of lost blue carbon wetlands by 2030.

PROJECT DRAWDOWN

Project Drawdown is a non-profit organization that conducts research and provides solutions to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and reverse global warming. They have identified and ranked 100 of the most effective climate solutions, such as renewable energy, regenerative agriculture, and reducing food waste, based on their potential to reduce emissions and their cost-effectiveness.
